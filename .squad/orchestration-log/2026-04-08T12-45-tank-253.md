## Key Decisions

### Icon Design: Green Dumbbell Vector

**Chosen:** Minimalist green dumbbell silhouette (#2ECC71 GymBro primary)

**Rationale:**
- Instantly recognizable as fitness app
- Scalable vector (no pixelation at any dpi)
- Matches GymBro brand identity (green for growth/progress)
- Differentiates from competitor icons (most use generic bars/graphs)
- Works in light and dark contexts

### Splash Screen: Material Design 3 Themed

**Chosen:** Android 12+ Material splash with dumbbell icon + fade-out animation

**Rationale:**
- Material Design 3 enforces branded splash on Android 12+
- Smooth brand presence during app launch (cold start 2-3s)
- Icon animation adds polish without slowing launch
- Backward-compatible: pre-Android-12 devices fall back to launcher icon

### Animation Duration

**Chosen:** 300ms fade-out animation

**Rationale:**
- Fast enough to feel responsive (avoids perception of delay)
- Slow enough to reinforce branding
- Aligns with Material Design motion guidelines (250-500ms)
