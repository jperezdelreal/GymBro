## Key Decisions

### Broad "Keep All" vs. Granular Optimization

**Chosen:** Comprehensive keep rules (164 lines)

**Rationale:**
- One missed rule → production crash (reflection, serialization, DI failures)
- APK size cost acceptable for MVP (5-10% larger than optimal)
- Team bandwidth better spent on features than R8 optimization
- Easy to maintain: rules organized by library with comments

**Alternatives Rejected:**
1. Granular `-keepclasseswithmembers` rules — too high risk of false negatives
2. `isMinifyEnabled = false` — exposes source structure, 3-5x APK bloat
3. Library-specific `consumer-rules.pro` — deferred to post-MVP

### Gradle Heap Allocation

**Chosen:** 4GB (`-Xmx4g`)

**Context:** Default 2GB insufficient for R8 processing large dependency graphs. Team reported OOM errors during release builds. Heap doubling aligns with Android Studio recommendations for complex projects.
